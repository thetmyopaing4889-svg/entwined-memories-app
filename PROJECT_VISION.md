Entwined Memories
Why this project exists
Entwined Memories was never created to become another gallery application.
It was never created to become another social media platform.
It was never created to become another cloud storage service.
It exists for only one reason.
To preserve the story of a little girl's life.
Every smile.
Every birthday.
Every tiny achievement.
Every ordinary day that one day will become an extraordinary memory.
Our Mission
One day, many years from now,
our daughter will open this app.
She will scroll through the memories.
She will watch the videos.
She will read every note written by her parents.
And hopefully she will smile and quietly think,
"My parents loved me so much."
If this happens,
Entwined Memories has fulfilled its purpose.
Design Philosophy
Technology is invisible.
Love is visible.
Every screen should feel warm.
Every interaction should feel gentle.
Every animation should feel peaceful.
This is not a productivity app.
This is not a media manager.
This is a memory home.
Core Principles
Family first.
Emotion before technology.
Memories before features.
Simplicity over complexity.
Privacy above everything.
The child is always the center.
What success means
Success is not downloads.
Success is not revenue.
Success is not popularity.
Success is a daughter smiling years later while reading the memories her parents left behind.
Message to Future Developers
If you ever contribute to this project,
remember that every line of code protects someone's memories.
Build carefully.
Design with kindness.
Never let technology become more important than love.
From the Creator
For my beloved daughter,
I cannot stop time.
But I hope I can preserve the moments that time leaves behind.
With all my love,
Dad ❤️
